Fluffy Pancakes

Home

Description

Light and fluffy homemade pancakes perfect for weekend breakfasts.

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Add wet ingredients and mix
  3. Pour ¼ cup portions onto hot griddle
  4. Flip when bubbles appear
